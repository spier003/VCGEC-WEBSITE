VCGEC Membership

Dec 6th 2021
  • Regular Members
    • Valenzuela City Government Employees
      • Permanent
      • Casual
      • Elective
      • Co-terminous and;
    • Permanent Employees of the national agencies detailed in Valenzuela City
  • Associate Member
    • Retired and/or resigned Valenzuela City government employees who opted to continue their membership.
    • Contractual employees

PRIVILEGES OF MEMBERS

  • A Regular Member shall have the following rights and privileges:
    • Attend during general membership meetings;
    • Avail of the services of the cooperative, subject to
      existing resolutions, rules and regulations, and certain
      conditions as may eb prescribed by the Board of Directors;
    • Inspect and examine the books of accounts, the minutes
      books, the share register; and other records of the
      Cooperative during reasonable office hours;
    • Secure copies of cooperative records/documents pertaining
      to the account information of the concerned member;
    • Participate in the continuing education and other training
      programs of the cooperative; and
    • Such other rights and privileges as may be granted by the General Assembly.
  • An Associate Member shall have the following rights and privileges:
    • Avail of the services of the Cooperative, subject to
      existing resolutions, rules and regulations, and certain
      conditions as may be prescribed by the Board of Directors
    • Inspect and examine the books of accounts, the minutes
      books, the share register; and other records of the
      Cooperative during reasonable office hours;
    • Has preference in the payment of interest on share capital
      and in the distributions of the net assets of the Cooperative
      in case of liquidation; and
    • Such other rights and privileges as may be granted by the General Assembly.
